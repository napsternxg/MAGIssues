In the above case the Journal ID `04C66E92` actually maps to `Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology`. 
However, the journal ID is mapped to the following names with the respective counts

```
Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology    4302
intelligence and security informatics                                   6
formal methods                                                          4
```

It appears that the journal name is incorrectly identified for in the above cases as the papers mentioned above actually belong to JASIST. 
While the 2005 version of JASIST was a special issue on `intelligence and security informatics`, the 2003 version wasn't a special issue in `formal methods`. Infact `formal methods` doesn't appear on any of the papers published in JASIST that year.
